# About Us Algebuckina Design is a small design and manufacturing company based in Western Sydney, Australia. Founded by Bradley Turner in 2021, our mission is to create innovative products that reduce waste and promote environmental responsibility. ### Our Vision We're committed to making a positive impact on the environment by designing and manufacturing products that are not only functional but also sustainable. Our goal is to reduce the amount of plastic used in household electronic peripherals and promote open-source design principles. ### What we offer We also offer a range of services to support our product development, including CAD design and drafting, rapid prototyping and small-scale production runs, high-quality rendering and visualization, UX/UI design and interactive 3D experiences and virtual reality environments. Whether you need help bringing a product to life, visualizing a design, or creating an immersive experience, we're here to help. ### What we are working on in house We're currently working on two exciting projects. Our first project is a wooden keyboard that combines a premium typing experience with a sustainable design. Our second project is an open-source car head unit that aims to keep older cars up to date with the latest technology while reducing electronic waste and promoting freedom from proprietary ecosystems. If you're interested in learning more about our products or services, please don't hesitate to contact us. We'd love to hear from you and explore how we can work together to create something amazing. # Services ### CAD Services We offer a range of CAD services. Our team of experienced designers can help you create accurate and detailed 3D models of your products or designs. ### Prototyping Services We provide rapid prototyping and small-scale production run services. Our team can help you create functional prototypes of your products or designs. ### Render Services We create high-quality renders of your products or designs, Our team can help you visualise your products or designs in a realistic and detailed way. ### Interaction Services We create immersive and interactive experiences. Our team can help you bring your products or designs to life in a fully immersive and interactive way. ### UX/UI Services We provide UX/UI design services. Our team can help you create user-friendly and intuitive interfaces for your products or designs.
