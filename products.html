Products

At Algebuckina Design, we're currently focused on providing services, but we’re also working on our own line of products using our in-house 3D printing and CNC capabilities. Openness is important to us — every product will be released under an MIT license once it reaches its End of Life (EoL). We're also committed to reducing our environmental footprint by offering replacement parts and using renewable materials wherever possible.

Cable Combs

Elevate your workspace or gaming setup with our Custom fit Cable Combs, designed to keep your cables tidy and organised while adding a touch of style. Unlike ordinary cable ties that can be bland and an eye sore, our Custom Cable Combs arrange each cable, creating a unique and visually appealing look. With the ability to customise the size and quantity to fit your specific needs, you can say goodbye to cable clutter and hello to a more streamlined and polished appearance. Our Custom Cable Combs are perfect for anyone looking to add a personal touch to their workspace, gaming station, or home office.

Sim Racing Button Pad

We’re currently developing a sim racing button pad designed for players who’ve simply run out of buttons. As racing sims get more complex, a wheel alone just doesn’t cut it — so we're building a standalone unit that gives you the extra controls you need without sacrificing immersion.

The button pad is being designed using mechanical keyboard switches and keycaps, giving it a tactile, responsive feel. The enclosure will be made from wood to reinforce our commitment to using renewable materials. We're also designing our own PCB in-house and using a Raspberry Pi Pico, so it'll be plug-and-play with most racing setups and sim platforms.
