Community

At Algebuckina Design, we're passionate about building a community of like-minded individuals who share our passion for design, innovation, and sustainability.

We're excited to host a range of community events, including:

- Sim Racing Tournaments
- Gaming Nights
- Behind The Scenes Content

Algebuckina Endurance Championship

The Algebuckina Endurance Championship (AEC) has been a long running Sim Racing Esports Series that started with Gran Turismo 4 in 2014 between a few friends and is slowly growing. We now run our yearly championship in Assetto Corsa with GT3 cars. Although it is invite only, we are looking to expand to the public in 2026 with a 1 off 24 hour multi class endurance event.

If you're interested in joining us in 2026, you can fill out the form here!

Gaming nights

We host monthly gaming nights on the second Monday of every month. These events provide an opportunity to foster the a community atmosphere that Algebuckina Design is striving for and will be free of charge. Our game selection focuses on free-to-play or affordable titles to ensure accessibility for all participants. Recent games played have included Team Fortress 2, Star Wars Battlefront 2 (2005), and Deadlock.

Join our discord for our next event!

Behind the Scenes Content

At Algebuckina Design, we want to be open about what we do and how we're doing it, so we aim to release behind the scenes content. This can be in the form of videos, blog posts or live streams that show you how our products are made. As part of any product purchased from Algebuckina Design, you'll get a custom 'how it was made' video or photo collection that shows the manufacturing process.
