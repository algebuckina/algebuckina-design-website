Algebuckina Endurance Championship

The Algebuckina Endurance Championship (AEC) has been a long running Sim Racing Esports Series that started with Gran Turismo 4 in 2014 between a few friends and is slowly growing. We now run our yearly championship in Assetto Corsa with GT3 cars. Although it is invite only, we are looking to expand to the public in 2026 with a 1 off 24 hour multi class endurance event.
