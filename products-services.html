Built from the ground up using WinForms, this tool provides a user-friendly interface for players to easily manage their mods. Key features of the mod manager include the ability to enable, disable, and reorganise mods, as well as import new mods to a designated 'mod' folder. The tool also reads and makes changes to the swgemu_live.cfg file, and creates a standalone mods.cfg file, ensuring simple integration with the game. In addition to mod management, the tool also functions as a SWGEmu Launcher, providing a convenient and centralised way for players to launch the game with their desired mods and settings. By simplifying the process of mod management, this tool aims to enhance the overall gaming experience for SWGEmu players. You can have a look at it [here](https://github.com/algebuckina/mtg-mod-manager). ### Saint Matthew's Uniting Church Livestreaming Solution We undertook a project to elevate the Church's livestreaming capabilities, providing a high-quality, broadcast experience for the community. The project involved multiple mini projects, including cameras, software, hardware and sound. To achieve this, we installed a three-camera setup, allowing for a more versatile and engaging visual experience. This was complemented by the design, build, and installation of a custom multimedia desk, which served as the central hub for the Church's streaming operations. The multimedia desk was integrated with OBS (Open Broadcasting Software) and a stream deck, enabling comprehensive control over the entire service. This setup allowed for switching between cameras, management of audio levels, and real-time monitoring of the stream, ensuring a smooth and uninterrupted experience for the online congregation. The project's impact was particularly significant during the Covid lockdowns in Sydney, as it enabled the Church to maintain a strong sense of community and connection with its members, even when in-person attendance was not possible. The new streaming setup allowed the Church to reach a wider audience, providing a vital lifeline for those who were unable to attend services in person.
